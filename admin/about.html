Our vision

We want to innovate the hotel experience bringing home automation into this business.

Description of the application

We use the following devices:

  • 2 4in1 sensors
  • 1 door magnetic sensor
  • 1 dimmer
  • 1 RGBW (unfortunately, this device "exploded" and we have to simulate its behavior)

We use these devices simulating a hotel room in this way:

  • The 4in1 located in the bathroom is going to be triggered when a person walks into the bathroom: the light will switch on (dimmer)
  • The door sensor, with the movement sensor of the 4in1, will start the towel warmer for the customer. This towel warmer should display a variety of colours from blue to red in the RGBW
  • Also, the other 4in1 located in the room, will detect if the humidity has increased too much. If so, it will trigger another dimmer (dehumidifier).

What we love about FI-WARE
